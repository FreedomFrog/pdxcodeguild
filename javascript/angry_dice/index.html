Angry Dice

A player needs two Angry Dice. Player rolls the dice, looking to complete Stage 1, then Stage 2, then Stage 3.

  1. STAGE 1
  2. One die showing 1 pip, another showing 2 pips.
  3. STAGE 2
  4. One die showing the Angry face (which represents a 3), another showing 4 pips.
  5. STAGE 3
  6. One die showing 5 pips, another showing 6 pips.

A player does not have to perfectly roll each Stage; if a die shows one face in a set, that die is locked (left the same) and the player now rolls the other die to complete the set.
